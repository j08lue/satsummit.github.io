## What it is

**SatSummit** is an event that gathers leaders in the satellite industry and experts in global development for 2 days of presentations and in-depth conversations on solving the world's most critical development challenges with satellite data.

From climate change to population growth to natural resource availability, earth observation data offers insights into today's biggest global issues.

## When and where

The second SatSummit gathering of 2024 will be held at the [**NOVA School of Business & Economics**](https://www.novasbe.unl.pt/en/about-us/contacts-directions) in Carcavelos, Lisbon, Portugal on November 18 and 19th.

## Why Lisbon

Lisbon, Portugal is an exciting and growing technology hub. As mainland Europe's westernmost capital, it is strategically situated on the Atlantic which makes it geographically accessible for attendees from Africa, the Americas, and the rest of Europe.  

We heard you when you asked for a more accessible location. Our goal is to provide our international community of satellite industry and global development experts more convenient and exciting locations to convene and work towards solutions for to solve the world's most critical development challenges with satellite data.

## Fulfillment policy

Please note that all ticket sales are final within 10 days of the event. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds during this period except under extraordinary circumstances, such as illness or airline-related issues. Should you encounter such a situation, we encourage you to reach out to us directly to discuss your situation at info@satsummit.io.

If your organization has purchased a ticket but you can no longer attend, tickets are transferrable to another individual within your organization. Please contact us if you need assistance with transferring your ticket.

We appreciate your understanding and look forward to your participation at the conference.

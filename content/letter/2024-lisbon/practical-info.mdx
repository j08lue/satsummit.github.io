## What it is

**SatSummit** is an event that gathers leaders in the satellite industry and experts in global development for 2 days of presentations and in-depth conversations on solving the world's most critical development challenges with satellite data.

## When and where

The 2024 Lisbon edition takes place in November 18 & 19 at [**NOVA SBE**](https://www.novasbe.unl.pt/en/about-us/contacts-directions), in Carcavelos - Lisbon.

## How to get to the venue

**NOVA SBE** is located at Rua da Holanda 1, 2775-405 Carcavelos.

## Fulfillment policy

Please note that all ticket sales are final within 10 days of the event. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds during this period except under extraordinary circumstances, such as illness or airline-related issues. Should you encounter such a situation, we encourage you to reach out to us directly to discuss your situation at info@satsummit.io.

If your organization has purchased a ticket but you can no longer attend, tickets are transferrable to another individual within your organization. Please contact us if you need assistance with transferring your ticket.

We appreciate your understanding and look forward to your participation at the conference.

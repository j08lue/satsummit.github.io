Anthony is a cloud engineer at Development Seed. He works with the Earthdata Infrastructure team, building tools and services to enable and promote scientific computing in the cloud. Anthony is passionate about building systems that allow decision-makers to have greater impact by better understanding massive amounts of earthdata.

Before joining the team, Anthony worked as a full-stack web developer providing solutions ranging from mapping property rights of marginalized populations, managing and understanding industrial imagery of remote locations, and managing bathymetric data in the cloud.

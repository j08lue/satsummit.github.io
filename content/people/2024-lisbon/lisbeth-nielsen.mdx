Lisbeth Nielsen is a data scientist and remote sensing specialist at DHI, focused on developing methods for mapping shallow coastal ecosystems and bathymetry using earth observation data. She works on developing efficient models suitable for monitoring submerged ecosystems to explore their dynamics on seasonal and interannual time scales. She has a background in geophysics.

Luis Pazos is a Staff Researcher at Degas, where he is leading the development of an agriculture-focused foundation model. In his previous appointment, he served as a Senior Researcher at Google, leading several AI projects in the agricultural domain. He focused on using remote sensing to monitor farm activity in Brazil and the US. Prior to this, he was a Heising Simons fellow at UC Berkeley, specializing in physics research. His work has appeared in leading journals, including those from the National Academy of Sciences, the Nature group and Science.

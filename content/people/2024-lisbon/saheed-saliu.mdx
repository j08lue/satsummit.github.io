Saheed Saliu is a consultant at Dev-Afrique Development Advisors and leads the geospatial practice for the African continent. With extensive experience in managing and manipulating geospatial data, acquiring GIS data, designing maps, measurement, and evaluation, and managing land use. Saheed is an expert in geospatial data management and analysis, physical planning policymaking, and land use planning and management.

Saheed is a certified Town Planner registered with the Town Planners Registration Council of Nigeria. He is also a member of the Nigerian Institute of Town Planners. He holds a master’s degree in Geographic Information Systems and a bachelor’s degree (with honors) in Urban and Regional Planning from the University of Lagos. He is currently based in Abuja, Nigeria.

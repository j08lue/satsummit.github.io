Ali is a Data Scientist at Fraym, a data company interested in providing insights to clients using household survey data, satellite imagery, and other modeled surfaces in a machine learning framework. On the team, Ali contributes to various research and development projects that focus on improving Fraym's machine learning methodologies. She has a particular interest in feature engineering and believes that a crucial component of any modeling workflow is using your inputs thoughtfully and artfully in order to yield the best results.

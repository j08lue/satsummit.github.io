Katie Tyler is the Customer Success & Geospatial Services Manager for Lens at Upstream Tech where she helps conservation-minded organizations leverage remote sensing for better outcomes. Prior to Upstream Tech, Katie spent some time working for the World Wildlife Fund and the National Park Service.

Katie received her Master of Arts in Geography with a concentration in GIS from the University of Colorado Boulder. She also holds a B.A. in Environmental Science from Mount Holyoke College.

Seamus Lombardo works on the Impact and Sustainability Team at Planet Labs, where he supports initiatives to employ satellite data in biodiversity conservation and environmental reporting. He also works to measure and improve the impact of Planet’s collaborations with governments, NGOs, and businesses. Seamus’s work and publications focus on satellite remote sensing for environmental and humanitarian applications. Previously, he completed his PhD at MIT researching remote sensing and integrated complex systems modeling to support sustainable development.

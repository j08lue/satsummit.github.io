About

SatSummit brings together satellite industry experts and global development leaders for in-depth conversations about how satellite imagery is solving some of the world's most critical development challenges. From climate change to population growth to natural resource availability, satellite data is offering insights into today's biggest global issues.

Join us as we explore current satellite data capabilities and global applications to identify real opportunities for partnership and innovation.

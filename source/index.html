About

SatSummit convenes leaders in the satellite industry and experts in global development for in-depth conversations on solving the world's most critical development challenges with satellite data. From climate change to population growth to natural resource availability, earth observation data offers insights into today's biggest global issues.

SatSummit is committed to exploring the current state of satellite data capabilities and new systems. Our second SatSummit explored the topics ranging from machine learning to automated monitoring to identifying specific opportunities for partnership and innovation. Read more about the presentations, program launches and commitments, and news related to SatSummit on our Insights blog, and stay tuned for more information on SatSummit 3!

Featured Sessions

  1. Planetary scale analysis

    Next generation datasets are cloud-based and analysis-ready, supportting planetary scale analysis, AI, and direct integration into web apps.

  2. Fast Food Security

    We have the data to support rapid calibration of crop health and yield, to precisely predict food crisis and to operationalize food intelligence to prevent hunger.

  3. Out of the Dark. What you learn from the world at night.

    It is remarkable what you can learn about the world at night. Forest fires, oil production, economic activity.. all shine at night.

  4. Bot or not?

    Industry leaders in applied AI discuss emerging design patterns in combining humans and machines to build a queryable planet.

  5. A pixel tells a thousand words

    Rockstar storytellers share how they're using maps and imagery to share compelling stories with audiences around the world.

  6. Anyone can go to space: the new space ecosystem

    Countries that have previously be unable to establish a national space program, now take advantage of partnerships to access satellites and sensors to protect their boarders, track their natural resources, and monitor human development.

  7. Seeing more than red, green and blue

    New satellites take us beyond the human eye - they see through the smoke of wildfires, detect and measure the gas leaking into the air from faulty pipelines, and capture the world at night.

  8. Eye on Gotham

    We finally have the spatial and temporal resolution to keep up with the cadence of a modern city. These presenters show you new ways to look at our urban landscape.

  9. 2020 Space Census

    Worldwide, the 2020 census cycle is our last chance to calibrate our data collection.

  10. Conservation Conversation. What's your relation?

    Cutting edge conservation experts discuss building realtime global monitoring for conservation operations and what other sectors can learn.

  11. Friends with benefits: Gov, Commercial, NGOs

    Masters of bureaucratic ju-jitsu show you how to get bureaucracy out of the way so that we can share data and work together on common goals across government, NGOs, and companies.

  12. Artificial Intelligence 101

    Peel away the hype and talk specifics on artificial intelligence for earth observations. What's the current state of the art and what can AI tell us?

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is SatSummit?

    SatSummit is a two-day conference that convenes leaders in the satellite industry and experts in global development for in-depth conversations on solving the world's most critical development challenges with satellite data. From climate change to population growth to natural resource availability, Earth observation data offers insights into today's biggest global issues.

  2. How do I register?

    You can register here. Onsite registration will not be available.

  3. Am I able to purchase tickets for only one day of the event?

    Yes, we have ticket levels that include access to both days of SatSummit. You can also purchase tickets to attend Day 1 or Day 2 only.

  4. Will you make special accommodations for attendees observing Yom Kippur?

    Please reach out to us directly at info@satsummit.io to discuss dietary needs, schedule, and other accommodations that we can assist with while you’re at SatSummit. If you are unable to attend Day 1 of SatSummit, we have separate tickets available for the Day 1 party at the Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum and for Day 2 of the summit. Please send your ticket request to info@satsummit.io.

  5. Where is SatSummit being held?

    SatSummit will take place at the Ronald Reagan Building and International Trade Center located at 1300 Pennsylvania Ave NW, Washington, DC 20004.

  6. Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

    The venue is fully ADA compliant. Should you need any accommodations to make this event accessible to you, please reach out to us at info@satsummit.io

  7. Is there a Mother’s room available if I am nursing?

    Yes, we have a private and comfortable space available for nursing Mother’s.

About

The satellite industry is rapidly advancing. New satellite and data processing capabilities unlock powerful insights into the earth, its climate, and its populations. The global development community stands to benefit immensely from these new capabilities. Development organizations will need to retool to take advantage of new capabilities.

The global development sector offers the satellite industry a multimillion dollar imagery market and the opportunity to help solve the worlds hardest problems. The industry will need creative new product and business offerings to effectively work with the development sector.

#SatSummit will bring together satellite industry leaders with innovative global development institutions. The one-day event will set the agenda for cooperation between the two communities. Sessions will cover business models for imagery in global development, analytical needs of the sector, imagery coordination for disaster response, and aligning licensing and procurement needs. Targeted short presentations will show capabilities that can provide massive insight into development and disaster response.
